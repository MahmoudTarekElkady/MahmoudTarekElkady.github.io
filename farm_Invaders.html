Farm Invaders
Alien Defense Farming

Protect your farm from alien invaders while planting crops, upgrading your farm, and fighting off increasingly challenging waves of enemies.

Key Features

  • Plant and harvest crops while defending your farm from alien waves.
  • Upgrade weapons and farming tools to enhance your survival chances.
  • Fight a variety of alien types, from basic invaders to challenging boss aliens.
  • Strategize with limited resources to balance farming and combat.
  • Upgrade farm tools and defensive items such as fences and turrets.
  • Progressively harder alien waves, each with unique and challenging enemies.

Development Tools

  • Unity Engine for gameplay development.
  • C# for scripting combat, farming mechanics, and UI interactions.
  • Asset creation using Photoshop and Blender for 2D art and animations.

Gameplay Overview

In *Farm Invaders*, you are a farmer fighting off waves of alien invaders while simultaneously planting crops, harvesting them for profit, and upgrading your farm. Each wave introduces new, tougher enemies, making it essential to manage both farming and combat effectively.

Alien Waves and Enemy Types

As the game progresses, aliens will attack in waves. Each wave introduces tougher and more numerous enemies, making it important to balance farming and defense strategies:

  • Basic Aliens: Move slowly, easy to defeat.
  • Flying Aliens: Move quickly, harder to hit.
  • Boss Aliens: Appear every few waves, requiring stronger weapons and strategy to defeat.

Economy and Upgrades

Money is earned by selling crops and killing aliens. You can use this money to buy seeds, new weapons, or upgrade your farming and defensive tools. Key aspects of the economy include:

  • Planting Seeds: Buy seeds to grow crops and harvest them for profit. Higher-tier crops take longer to grow but yield more money.
  • Weapons: Starting with basic weapons like a shotgun, you can upgrade to machine guns, flamethrowers, and laser rifles. Weapons require ammo, which can be bought with in-game currency.
  • Farm Upgrades: Invest in farm upgrades such as larger planting areas, faster-growing crops, or automated harvesting tools.
  • Defensive Items: You can buy traps, fences, turrets, and other items to help protect your farm while you focus on farming.

Survival Strategy

The key to surviving longer in *Farm Invaders* is managing your time and resources. Balancing farming tasks, upgrading your farm, and preparing for alien waves is essential for success. Some of the challenges you'll face include:

  • Time Management: Spend too much time farming, and you may not have time to defend against alien waves. Spend too much time fighting, and you won't earn enough money to upgrade your farm or weapons.
  • Limited Resources: Weapons and ammo require money, and you need to balance spending between farming and defense. Some weapons are more efficient against specific alien types, making it essential to plan ahead.

Possible Extensions

  • Co-op Mode: Two players can work together to split tasks between farming and combat, creating a more dynamic experience.
  • Boss Battles: Every 10th wave introduces a massive alien boss with unique abilities, requiring careful strategy to defeat.
  • Seasons and Weather: Different conditions affect crop growth or alien behavior, such as varying seasons, rain, or fog.
