Augmented Reality MMORPG Project

Step into an immersive augmented reality experience where players explore vast open worlds, engage in dynamic battles, and forge their unique paths. This MMORPG uses cutting-edge AI, including agents and deep reinforcement learning, to create responsive NPC behavior, ensuring a constantly evolving and realistic gameplay experience.

Gameplay Showcase

Project Details

This augmented reality MMORPG is designed to provide an unparalleled player experience:
  • Augmented Reality Integration: The game uses augmented reality to bring the game world into the real world, allowing players to interact with their surroundings in new and exciting ways.
  • AI-Driven NPC Behavior: Agents powered by AI control NPC actions, making them responsive and adaptable to the player's actions.
  • Deep Reinforcement Learning: NPCs learn and improve their behavior over time through deep reinforcement learning algorithms, making the world feel more alive and dynamic.
  • Real-Time Combat: Engage in fluid, action-packed combat, where AI-driven enemies learn from player strategies and adapt to provide a more challenging experience.
  • Open-World Exploration: Navigate a vast, immersive world with cities, forests, and dungeons, all seamlessly integrated into the augmented reality environment.
  • Multiplayer Features: Connect with other players in real time to collaborate or compete in epic adventures.

Tools and Technologies Used

  • Unity Engine: The primary platform for developing the game world and systems, including augmented reality integration.
  • C# Programming: Implemented mechanics, game logic, and multiplayer functionality.
  • Blender: Created high-quality 3D assets, including characters and environments.
  • Deep Reinforcement Learning: Used advanced machine learning algorithms to control NPC behavior, allowing them to adapt and improve over time.
  • AI Agents: Implemented AI agents to handle NPC interactions and decision-making processes.
  • ARKit / ARCore: Integrated augmented reality features for seamless interaction between the game world and the real world.
  • Photon Networking: Integrated multiplayer networking to enable seamless online gameplay with other players in real time.
