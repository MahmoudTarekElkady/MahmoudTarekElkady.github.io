MMORPG Project

Step into an immersive multiplayer experience where players can explore massive open worlds, engage in epic battles, and forge their unique paths. This MMORPG is designed to bring life to player interactions, dynamic environments, and rich storylines.

Gameplay Showcase

Project Details

This MMORPG project is crafted to offer players an engaging and expansive experience:

  • Open-World Exploration: Navigate breathtaking landscapes, from dense forests to bustling cities.
  • Dynamic NPC Interactions: Engage with non-playable characters who react based on player decisions.
  • Quests and Objectives: Complete thrilling quests ranging from small tasks to epic adventures.
  • Real-Time Combat System: Experience fluid and responsive combat mechanics, including weapon switching and skill casting.
  • Multiplayer Features: Collaborate with or compete against other players in real time.
  • Customizable Avatars: Design unique characters with a variety of cosmetic and functional options.

Tools and Technologies Used

  • Unity Engine: The primary platform for developing the game world and systems.
  • C# Programming: Implemented mechanics, game logic, and multiplayer functionality.
  • Blender: Created high-quality 3D assets, including characters and environments.
  • Photon Networking: Integrated multiplayer networking for seamless online gameplay.
