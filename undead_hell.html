Undead Hell Game

A thrilling survival game where you face hordes of skeletons spawned by an evil Necromancer.

Gameplay Demo

Overview

"Undead Hell" is an intense survival game where players must fight off waves of skeletons, all controlled by a powerful Necromancer. Your goal is to survive the onslaught and defeat the Necromancer to end the reign of the undead.

Key Features

  • Battle against waves of undead skeletons spawned by an evil Necromancer.
  • Strategic combat with a variety of weapons and tactics.
  • Immersive atmosphere filled with tension and action.

Development Tools

  • Phaser for game development.
  • JavaScript for gameplay programming.
  • Blender for creating assets and textures.
