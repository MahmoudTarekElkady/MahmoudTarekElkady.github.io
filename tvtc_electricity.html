Details

Developed for the Technical and Vocational Training Corporation (TVTC), this immersive VR simulator is designed to guide students and electrical technicians through safety training and panel operations in a completely risk-free virtual environment.

Key Features

  • AI Assistant Robot Guide: Features a fully animated assistant robot that dynamically instructs the player, points out targets, and gives real-time visual and audio feedback.
  • High-Fidelity Hand Interactions: Integrated realistic physics-based controller interactions for electrical wiring, flipping heavy breakers, and fitting fuses.
  • Bilingual Localization: Full translation and voiceover localization in both Arabic and English.
  • Safety Protocol Enforcement: Evaluates the user's adherence to safety standards (PPE checks, breaker isolation, voltage testing using meters).

Tools Used

  • Unity Engine (Game Engine)
  • XR Interaction Toolkit & OpenXR (VR Interaction Framework)
  • C# Scripting (Systems Architecture, Localization & State Machine Logic)
